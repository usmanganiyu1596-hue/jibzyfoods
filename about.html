About JIBZYFOODS

JIBZYFOODS is a family-run business specializing in processed African food products — dried catfish, dried snail, crayfish, and dried pepper. We process, clean, and package our products to ensure freshness and convenience for your kitchen.

Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable food staples to households and businesses across the region. We source responsibly and follow safe processing practices.
